Our Mission

WHY THIS MOVEMENT EXISTS

In 2023, President Bola Ahmed Tinubu took office with bold promises and a Renewed Hope Agenda. Millions of Nigerians deserve to know — clearly, honestly, and accessibly — what has been delivered on those promises.

Tinubu Delivers is not a government agency. We are citizens holding power accountable. We track real projects, real numbers, and real outcomes — then put that information directly in the hands of Nigerians through the most accessible technology available: a QR code.

01
Track & Verify
We monitor federal government delivery across 12 sectors and all 36 states, powered by live AI-assisted research.
02
Amplify
We equip approved supporter groups with unique QR codes to put information directly in the hands of Nigerians — on the streets, in markets, on campuses.
03
Measure
Every QR code scan is tracked. Every group's reach is measured. We know what works and where Nigerians are most engaged.
04
Mobilise
From student unions to market associations, we build a ground-level movement that connects citizens to the evidence of governance.

How It Works

THE QR CODE MOVEMENT

1
Apply to Join
Your organisation registers on this platform. Tell us who you are, where you operate, and how you plan to deploy.
2
Get Approved
The Tinubu Delivers team reviews your application. Approved groups receive a unique QR code linked only to your organisation.
3
Deploy
Print your QR code on t-shirts, stickers, and fliers — or share digitally. Every scan is tracked to your group.
4
Citizens Engage
Anyone who scans lands on a live, AI-powered platform showing federal achievements by sector and by their state.
5
We Measure Impact
The movement team tracks which groups are driving the most engagement and provides support to the most active.
